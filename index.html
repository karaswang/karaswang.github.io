I'm Kara Wang, a junior at Westwood High School in Austin, Texas. I believe the most meaningful work happens at the intersection of disciplines — where computational chemistry meets gene therapy, where violin performance meets community service, where data analysis meets global health education.

Whether I'm debugging ML models at UT Austin's High School Research Academy, performing as first chair in my orchestra at Lincoln Center, or co-designing health education curricula for communities in Nigeria — I bring the same principle: show up fully, think deeply, and serve others.
